What is Formly

`ngx-formly` is an Angular module which has a Components to help customize and render JavaScript/JSON configured forms. The `formly-form` Component and the `FormlyConfig` service are very powerful and bring unmatched maintainability to your applications forms.

Simpler than cake

Do it yourself templates through `formlyConfig` service, and fully customizable for when you need to stay on-brand or just have extra field type.

One developer, many frameworks

A single Web development team is now a cross-framework application powerhouse. Focus more energy on building better experiences, and less energy on making experiences consistent across development teams and frameworks.
